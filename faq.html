Frequently Asked Questions

What are your operating hours?

We’re open Thursday–Tuesday from 8 AM–4 PM (Saturday & Sunday 9 AM–3 PM) and closed on Monday.

Do you offer vegan or gluten-free options?

Yes! Most of our bowls and smoothies can be customized to be vegan or gluten-free. Just ask our team!

Can I place an order online?

Absolutely! You can order directly from our website or visit popular delivery platforms. For large party orders, contact us directly.

Do you offer party or catering services?

Yes, we do! Vibrant Fusion can cater for birthdays, events, and gatherings. Reach out via our Contact page to plan your order.

Where are you located?

We’re at 1175 Barrington Rd. Hoffman Estates, IL 60169. View our location to get directions easily.
